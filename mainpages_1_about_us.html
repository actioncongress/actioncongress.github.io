Our Organization

As we expand, our objectives as a student-run, community based organization is to provide to others purpose through activities designed at strengthening community awareness and promoting personal growth. Action Congress aims at supporting the community through civil discussion and by hosting team-building events. Through conscious reflection of our behavior and our values, we strive to uplift the emotional and mental fortitude of our members, instilling into them confidence and self-awareness through civil discussion and participation in community-building events.
