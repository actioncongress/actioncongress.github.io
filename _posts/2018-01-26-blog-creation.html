Welcome to the first blog post of the Action Congress Organization!

It is the goal of our organization to create content available quickly and conveniently. Blogger is an appropriate asset for doing just that, which is why this post coming to you right now, dear reader, is powered through Blogger.

Blogger is now a service offered by Google, which means important Action Congress members can easily access and edit the posts through the services already available to them, seeing as they have Google accounts.

Blog posts on Blogger can be easily edited later for a cleaner look or new information. They will be updated and reflected on our website in real time!

The posts we publish using Blogger can also be implemented directly into other domains and works well with HTML and CSS. Think of Blogger as the Google Drive of the website.

Lastly, Blogger is entirely free! With this web-based software available to us, we will be up and running in no time.

Thank you for reading.
