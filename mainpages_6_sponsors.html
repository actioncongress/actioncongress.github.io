How to Become a Sponsor

To get in contact with the organization, contact Ally Heuer, Director of Public Outreach, at ally@actioncongress.org

To reach us regarding business or sponsorship particularly, contact Ryan Sherring, Director of External Affairs, at ryan@actioncongress.org

To reach a specific member, send an email to their first name, plus the extension .actioncongress.org


Our Patreon

If you wish to support our organization, but don't have a company or similar entity to stand in for you, you can donate directly to our Patreon account.
